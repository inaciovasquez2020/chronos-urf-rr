# H4.1 unconditional closure blocker

Status: Open.

The finite-patch H4.1 chain is already reduced to one terminal statement:

```text
FGL(k,R,B)
Terminal statement
For each fixed finite parameter triple (k,R,B), every zero-mean FO^k-visible function on the finite patch space decomposes into the span of bounded-support dual parity characters.
Equivalently:
V_{k,R,B} ⊆ span(constants ∪ bounded-support dual parity characters)
Consequence
If FGL(k,R,B) is proved for every fixed (k,R,B), then every nontrivial zero-mean FO^k-visible local difference induces a bounded-support dual dependency, and the finite-patch form of H4.1 is unconditional.
Boundary
No unconditional theorem-level closure is claimed until FGL(k,R,B) is proved.
