# H4.1 finite-patch promotion

## Status
Conditional.

Assume:
\[
\forall b\in \mathcal B_{k,R,B}\setminus U_{k,R,B},\quad
\exists u\in U_{k,R,B}\text{ such that }\langle b,u\rangle\neq 0.
\]

Then:
\[
V_{k,R,B}\cap U_{k,R,B}^{\perp}=\{0\}.
\]

Hence:
\[
V_{k,R,B}\subseteq U_{k,R,B}=W_{k,R,B}\oplus \langle 1\rangle.
\]

Therefore every FO\(^k\)-visible local difference on \(X(\mathcal P_{k,R,B})\) induces either:
1. a nonzero constant-mode separation, or
2. a nontrivial bounded-support dual parity separation.

After quotienting out the constant mode, every nontrivial FO\(^k\)-visible local difference induces a bounded-support dual dependency.

## Promotion target
Finite-patch Fourier completeness promotes to the finite-patch form of H4.1.
