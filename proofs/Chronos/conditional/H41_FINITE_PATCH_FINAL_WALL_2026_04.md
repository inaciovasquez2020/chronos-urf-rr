# H4.1 finite-patch final wall

## Status
Conditional.

The remaining unproved statement is:

\[
\forall b\in \mathcal B_{k,R,B}\setminus \bigl(W_{k,R,B}\oplus \langle 1\rangle\bigr),\quad
\exists \chi\in W_{k,R,B}\ \text{such that}\ \langle b,\chi\rangle\neq 0
\ \lor\ 
\langle b,1\rangle\neq 0.
\]

Equivalently:

Every nonzero FO\(^k\)-local basis element outside the bounded-support dual-character span plus constants has nonzero correlation with a bounded-support dual parity character or with the constant function.

## Terminal obstruction
Finite-patch Fourier completeness for FO\(^k\)-local basis elements.

## Closure consequence
Once proved,
\[
V_{k,R,B}\subseteq W_{k,R,B}\oplus \langle 1\rangle,
\]
and the finite-patch form of H4.1 is unconditional.
