# H4.1 finite-patch objects

## Status
Conditional.

## Fixed parameters
Let \(k,R,B \in \mathbb{N}\) be fixed.

## Canonical patch class
Let \(\mathcal P_{k,R,B}\) denote the finite class of affine-lift \(K_4\) patch unions consisting of at most \(B\) rooted radius-\(R\) patches.

## History space
Let
\[
X(\mathcal P_{k,R,B})
\]
denote the finite set of admissible histories on members of \(\mathcal P_{k,R,B}\).

## FO\(^k\)-local observable space
Let
\[
V_{k,R,B} \subseteq \mathbb{R}^{X(\mathcal P_{k,R,B})}
\]
be the linear span of all FO\(^k\)-local observables on \(X(\mathcal P_{k,R,B})\).

## Bounded-support dual-character space
Let
\[
W_{k,R,B} \subseteq \mathbb{R}^{X(\mathcal P_{k,R,B})}
\]
be the linear span of all bounded-support dual parity characters on \(X(\mathcal P_{k,R,B})\).

## Constant line
Let
\[
\langle 1\rangle \subseteq \mathbb{R}^{X(\mathcal P_{k,R,B})}
\]
denote the one-dimensional subspace spanned by the constant function \(1\).

## Target inclusion
\[
V_{k,R,B} \subseteq W_{k,R,B} \oplus \langle 1\rangle.
\]
