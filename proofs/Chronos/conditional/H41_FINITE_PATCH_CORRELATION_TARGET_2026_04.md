# H4.1 finite-patch correlation target

## Status
Conditional.

For fixed \(k,R,B \in \mathbb N\), let
\[
\mathcal B_{k,R,B}
\]
be a basis of \(V_{k,R,B}\), and let
\[
U_{k,R,B}:=W_{k,R,B}\oplus \langle 1\rangle.
\]

The remaining target is:

\[
\forall b\in \mathcal B_{k,R,B}\setminus U_{k,R,B},\quad
\exists u\in U_{k,R,B}\text{ such that }\langle b,u\rangle\neq 0.
\]

Equivalent split form:

\[
\forall b\in \mathcal B_{k,R,B}\setminus U_{k,R,B},\quad
\big(\langle b,1\rangle\neq 0\big)\ \lor\
\exists \chi\in W_{k,R,B}\text{ such that }\langle b,\chi\rangle\neq 0.
\]

## Consequence
This implies
\[
V_{k,R,B}\cap U_{k,R,B}^{\perp}=\{0\},
\]
hence
\[
V_{k,R,B}\subseteq W_{k,R,B}\oplus \langle 1\rangle.
\]
