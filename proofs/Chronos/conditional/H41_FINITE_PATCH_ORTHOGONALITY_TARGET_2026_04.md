# H4.1 finite-patch orthogonality target

## Status
Conditional.

For fixed \(k,R,B \in \mathbb N\), equip
\[
\mathbb R^{X(\mathcal P_{k,R,B})}
\]
with the standard inner product
\[
\langle f,g\rangle := \sum_{x\in X(\mathcal P_{k,R,B})} f(x)g(x).
\]

Define
\[
U_{k,R,B}:=W_{k,R,B}\oplus \langle 1\rangle.
\]

Define the orthogonal complement
\[
U_{k,R,B}^{\perp}:=\{f\in \mathbb R^{X(\mathcal P_{k,R,B})}:\langle f,u\rangle=0\ \forall u\in U_{k,R,B}\}.
\]

## Orthogonality target
\[
V_{k,R,B}\cap U_{k,R,B}^{\perp}=\{0\}.
\]

## Consequence
\[
V_{k,R,B}\subseteq U_{k,R,B}=W_{k,R,B}\oplus \langle 1\rangle.
\]
