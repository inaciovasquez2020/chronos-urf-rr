# Bounded Treewidth Decomposition Property

## Claim
Graphs of bounded treewidth satisfy the decomposition property.

## Formal
For each treewidth bound `t` and radius `R`, there exists `C(t,R)` such that for every graph `G` with
`treewidth(G) <= t`, every cycle `γ ∈ Z_1(G)` admits a decomposition

`γ = Σ_i γ_i + η`

where each `γ_i` has length at most `2R+1`, and the residual space generated by all such `η` has
dimension at most `C(t,R)`.

## Consequence
`dim(Z_1(G)/Z_1^{≤ 2R+1}(G)) <= C(t,R)`.
