# Non-Factorization Scope Boundary

## Positive Regime
For fixed t:
tw(G) ≤ t ⇒ Z1(G) is generated by bounded-radius local cycles.

## Negative Target Regime
Any URF non-factorization theorem must be witnessed on a family with:
tw(G_n) → ∞.

## Separation Template
Find (G_n^+, G_n^-) such that:
- local radius-R types agree
- global quotient-cycle invariant differs
- treewidth is unbounded
