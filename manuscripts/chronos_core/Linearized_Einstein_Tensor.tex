\section{Linearized Einstein Tensor}

\[
G_{\mu\nu}^{(1)}=-\tfrac{1}{2}\Box\bar h_{\mu\nu},
\]
The three divergence terms vanish by the Lorenz condition, so
\[
\Box\bar h_{\mu\nu}=-16\pi G\,T_{\mu\nu}.
\]

\[
h_{ij} = -2\Phi\,\delta_{ij}.
\]

\[
\Gamma^i_{00}=\tfrac{1}{2}g^{i\sigma}(2\partial_0 g_{\sigma 0}-\partial_\sigma g_{00})
=-\tfrac{1}{2}g^{ij}\partial_j g_{00}+O(\epsilon^2)
=-\tfrac{1}{2}\delta^{ij}\partial_j(-1-2\Phi)+O(\epsilon^2)
=\partial^i\Phi+O(\epsilon^2),
\]
where the \(g^{i0}\) term vanishes because \(\partial_0 g_{00}=0\), and the correction from \(g^{ij}-\delta^{ij}\) is \(O(\epsilon^2)\).
