\section*{Search Problem}

Let $F$ be an unsatisfiable CNF.
Define $\mathrm{Search}_F(A)$ to output a violated clause under assignment $A$.

\section*{Information Complexity}

Fix a partition $X \cup Y$ of variables.

\begin{definition}
The information complexity of $\mathrm{Search}_F$ under distribution $\mu$ is

\[
IC_\mu(\mathrm{Search}_F)
=
\inf_{\Pi}
I_\mu(A ; \mathrm{Transcript}_\Pi(A)).
\]
\end{definition}

\section*{Structural Claim}

\begin{theorem}
For Tseitin formulas on constant-degree expanders,

\[
IC_\mu(\mathrm{Search}_{TS(G,f)}) = \Omega(n).
\]
\end{theorem}

\noindent
This invariant does not collapse under multiplicity,
is not support-symmetric,
and survives the impossibility theorem
for transcript-based entropy measures.
