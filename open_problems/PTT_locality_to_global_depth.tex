\section{Propagation Transfer Theorem (PTT): Locality $\Rightarrow$ Global Depth}

\paragraph{Statement (PTT).}
In bounded-degree CNFs, if a derivation process is FO$^k$-local at each step and avoids any timestep
with simultaneous $\mathbb F_2$-span $\Omega(n)$, then the total derivation depth is $\Omega(n)$.

\paragraph{Status / gap.}
Requires a composition theorem showing FO$^k$ locality blocks long-range shortcutting while the span bound
blocks aggregation; their conjunction forces linear depth.

\paragraph{Closure target ingredient.}
A lifting/communication-style composition argument over expander gadgets that converts local indistinguishability
and low span into an additive depth lower bound.
