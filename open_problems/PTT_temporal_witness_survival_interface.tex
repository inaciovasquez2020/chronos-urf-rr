\section{PTT Temporal Witness Survival Interface}

\paragraph{Scope.}
This artifact records the weakest temporal witness interface required before
any layer-rank transfer argument. It does not prove PTT.

\paragraph{Target interface.}
For a resolution derivation $D$ and finite cut witness family $F$:

\[
\mathrm{AppearsInDerivation}(D,F)
\Longrightarrow
\exists t,\,
\mathrm{NontrivialLayerWitness}(D,t,F).
\]

\paragraph{Required future lemma.}
A derivation-time witness theorem connecting finite Tseitin cut witnesses
to a single derivation configuration.

\paragraph{Non-claims.}
This interface does not prove rank amplification, does not prove layer-rank
transfer, does not prove the synchronized-span obstruction, and does not
prove PTT.
