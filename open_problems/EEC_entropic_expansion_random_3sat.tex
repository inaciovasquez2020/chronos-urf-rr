\section{Entropic Expansion Conjecture (EEC) for Random 3-SAT}

\paragraph{Statement (EEC).}
For random $3$-SAT at density $m=cn$ with $c>c_0$, for every partial assignment fixing at most $\alpha n$
variables, the conditional entropy of satisfying assignments decreases by at most $O(1)$ bits per refinement
step unless $\Omega(n)$ variables are simultaneously constrained.

\paragraph{Status / gap.}
Clustering/OGP-type structure results do not directly yield an $O(1)$ per-step entropy-loss ceiling under
local refinement, especially in the presence of propagation cascades.

\paragraph{Closure target ingredient.}
A planted-to-random coupling plus a quantitative entropy-stability estimate under small restrictions,
or a replacement invariant that survives unit-propagation cascades.
