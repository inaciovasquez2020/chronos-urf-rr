\section{PTT Locality-to-Depth Obligation Surface}

\paragraph{Scope.}
This artifact records a bounded dependency obligation for the Propagation Transfer
Theorem (PTT). It does not prove the theorem.

\paragraph{Target obligation.}
A future composition lemma must connect:

\[
\text{FO}^{k}\text{-local derivation steps}
+
\text{bounded simultaneous } \mathbb{F}_2\text{-span}
\]

to an additive derivation depth lower bound.

\paragraph{Required missing ingredient.}
A lifting/composition argument over bounded-degree expander gadget families.

\paragraph{Non-claims.}
This surface does not prove PTT, does not prove a locality-to-depth theorem,
and does not construct the missing composition argument.
