\section{PTT Locality-to-Depth Composition Invariant Surface}

\paragraph{Scope.}
This artifact records the missing intermediate invariant required by the
Propagation Transfer Theorem (PTT). It does not prove PTT.

\paragraph{Invariant target.}
A future composition lemma must preserve the dependency:

\[
\mathrm{LocalFO}^k
\land
\mathrm{BoundedSpan}
\Longrightarrow
\mathrm{NoShortcutDepthCollapse}.
\]

\paragraph{Required inputs.}
\begin{itemize}
\item FO$^k$ locality certificate for each derivation step.
\item Simultaneous $\mathbb F_2$-span bound certificate.
\item Composition rule over bounded-degree expander gadget families.
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Missing lemma.}
A composition invariant showing that local indistinguishability and bounded
span prevent shortcut aggregation.

\paragraph{Non-claims.}
This surface does not prove the missing lemma, does not prove PTT, and does not
establish a derivation depth lower bound.
