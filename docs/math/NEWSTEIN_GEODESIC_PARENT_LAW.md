# Newstein Geodesic-Parent Law

Status: OPEN

## Statement
For every \(w \in B_R(r)\) with \(w \neq r\), the parent vertex \(\eta(w)\) lies on a shortest path from \(w\) to \(r\).

## Equivalent metric form
For every \(w \in B_R(r)\) with \(w \neq r\),
\[
d(w,r)=1+d(\eta(w),r).
\]

## Role
This is the weakest sufficient remaining ingredient for the one-step parent-depth decrement proof shell.
