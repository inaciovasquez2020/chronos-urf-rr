# Newstein Triangle-Vanishing Lemma

## Status
Conditional target.

## Lemma
For every vertex \(x\in V(B_n)\) and every triangle \(\tau\subseteq B_r^{B_n}(x)\),
\[
\phi_H(\partial\tau)=0.
\]

## Consequence
Together with the Newstein local triangle-generation lemma, this implies
\[
\phi_H(C)=0
\quad\text{for every cycle }C\subseteq B_r^{B_n}(x),
\]
hence the Newstein local cycle-vanishing lemma.

## Remaining proof obligations
1. identify the three lifted edge parities around each local triangle;
2. verify the twisted cocycle has even total parity on every triangle boundary;
3. formalize additivity of \(\phi_H\) on \(\mathbb F_2\)-boundary sums.

## Overclaim guard
No proof of the lemma is claimed here.
