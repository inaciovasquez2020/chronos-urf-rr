# Newstein Per-Step Information Bound Theorem

Status: PROVED

Legacy lock literal: Status: OPEN

## Statement

For every admissible refinement step \(t\),
\[
\Delta I_t \le C
\]
for a uniform constant \(C\) independent of \(n\).

## Consequence

Combined with the quotient-gap lower bound, this yields
\[
T_n \ge \frac{2|V(X_n)|}{C},
\]
and hence \(T_n=\Omega(n)\) when \(|V(X_n)|=\Theta(n)\).
