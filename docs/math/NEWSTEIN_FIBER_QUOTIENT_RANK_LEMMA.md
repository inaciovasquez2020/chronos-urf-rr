# Newstein Fiber Quotient-Rank Lemma

## Status
Conditional target.

## Lemma
Let \(T\) be a triangulated torus gadget, and let \(W\) denote the \(\mathbb F_2\)-span of the lifted triangle boundaries.

### Trivial lift
If \(\widetilde T^{\mathrm{triv}}\) is the trivial \(2\)-lift of \(T\), then
\[
\dim_{\mathbb F_2}\!\bigl(Z_1(\widetilde T^{\mathrm{triv}})/W^{\mathrm{triv}}\bigr)=4.
\]

### Twisted lift
If \(\widetilde T^{\mathrm{tw}}\) is a connected twisted \(2\)-lift of \(T\) obtained by odd monodromy on exactly one primitive torus direction and even monodromy on the other, then
\[
\dim_{\mathbb F_2}\!\bigl(Z_1(\widetilde T^{\mathrm{tw}})/W^{\mathrm{tw}}\bigr)=2.
\]

## Interpretation
The trivial lift contributes two extra quotient-cycle classes relative to the twisted lift on each torus fiber.

## Remaining proof obligations
1. identify \(Z_1/W\) with first \(\mathbb F_2\)-homology of the lifted torus surface;
2. compute \(H_1\) of the disjoint union of two tori;
3. compute \(H_1\) of the connected twisted double cover;
4. verify the chosen twist gives connected cover with one odd and one even primitive direction.

## Overclaim guard
No proof of the lemma is claimed here.
