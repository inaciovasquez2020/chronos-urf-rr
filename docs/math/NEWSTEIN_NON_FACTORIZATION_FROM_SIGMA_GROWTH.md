# Newstein Non-Factorization from Sigma Growth

Status: OPEN

## Statement

Assume the Global Quotient Lower Bound from Sigma Fibers:
\[
\dim_{\mathbf F_2}(Z_1/W^{\mathrm{glob}})\ge 2|U|
\]
for every family \(U\) of pairwise distinct sigma-fibers.

Assume \(|U|\to\infty\) along the chosen graph family.

Then the global quotient dimension is unbounded:
\[
\dim_{\mathbf F_2}(Z_1/W^{\mathrm{glob}})\to\infty.
\]

Consequently, the quotient invariant \(Q\) cannot factor through any bounded local type space, in particular not through rooted radius-\(r\) type and not through \(FO^k_r\)-type.

## Role

This is the exact non-factorization consequence extracted from linear sigma-fiber growth in the global quotient.
