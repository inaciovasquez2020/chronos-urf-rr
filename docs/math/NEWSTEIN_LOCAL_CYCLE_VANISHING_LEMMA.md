# Newstein Local Cycle-Vanishing Lemma

## Status
Conditional target.

## Lemma
For every vertex \(x\in V(B_n)\) and every cycle \(C\subseteq B_r^{B_n}(x)\),
\[
\phi_H(C)=0.
\]

It is sufficient to prove:

1. every cycle contained in \(B_r^{B_n}(x)\) is generated over \(\mathbb F_2\) by local triangle boundaries;
2. \(\phi_H(\partial\tau)=0\) for every triangle \(\tau\subseteq B_r^{B_n}(x)\).

Then \(\phi_H(C)=0\) for every cycle \(C\subseteq B_r^{B_n}(x)\).

## Consequence
The local cycle-vanishing lemma discharges the remaining hypothesis of the Newstein rooted-ball assembly lemma.

## Remaining proof obligations
1. prove every radius-\(r\) ball is planar/simply connected inside each torus gadget under \(L>2r+1\);
2. prove the expander backbone contributes no cycle inside radius \(r\) under girth \(>2r+1\);
3. prove every local cycle is an \(\mathbb F_2\)-sum of triangle boundaries;
4. verify the twisted cocycle vanishes on every triangle boundary.

## Overclaim guard
No proof of the lemma is claimed here.
