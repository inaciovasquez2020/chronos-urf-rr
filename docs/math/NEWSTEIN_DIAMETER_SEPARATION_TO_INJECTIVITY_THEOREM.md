# Newstein Diameter-Separation to Injectivity Theorem

Status: OPEN

## Statement

Assume the Diameter-Separation Filling Obstruction.

Let
\[
[c_u]\in Z_1(B_r(u);\mathbf F_2)/W_u,
\qquad
[c_v]\in Z_1(B_r(v);\mathbf F_2)/W_v
\]
be classes from distinct fibers \(u\neq v\).

If
\[
\iota_u([c_u])=\iota_v([c_v])
\]
in the global quotient, then
\[
[c_u]=0=[c_v].
\]

Equivalently, the induced map
\[
\bigoplus_{u}\bigl(Z_1(B_r(u);\mathbf F_2)/W_u\bigr)\longrightarrow Z_1/W^{\mathrm{glob}}
\]
is injective on distinct fiber summands.

## Role

This is the exact theorem-level conversion from the diameter-separation obstruction to cross-fiber injectivity.
