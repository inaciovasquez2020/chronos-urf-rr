# Newstein Rooted-Ball Assembly Lemma

## Status
Conditional target.

## Lemma
Assume:

1. the Newstein local coboundary criterion;
2. for every vertex \(x\in V(B_n)\),
   \[
   \phi_H(C)=0
   \quad\text{for every cycle } C \subseteq B_r^{B_n}(x).
   \]

Then for every \(x\), there exists
\[
f_x:V(B_r^{B_n}(x))\to\mathbb F_2
\]
such that
\[
\phi_H|_{B_r^{B_n}(x)}=\delta f_x.
\]

Consequently,
\[
\widetilde B_r^{\,\mathrm{tw}}(x)\cong \widetilde B_r^{\,\mathrm{triv}}(x).
\]

Hence,
\[
\operatorname{Type}_{k,r}(G_n)=\operatorname{Type}_{k,r}(H_n).
\]

## Consequence
The rooted-ball assembly lemma reduces rooted-ball trivialization to a local cycle-vanishing statement on every radius-\(r\) ball.

## Remaining proof obligations
1. verify cycle-vanishing of \(\phi_H\) on every radius-\(r\) ball;
2. invoke the local coboundary criterion ballwise;
3. transfer local lift isomorphism to equality of rooted radius-\(r\) types.
