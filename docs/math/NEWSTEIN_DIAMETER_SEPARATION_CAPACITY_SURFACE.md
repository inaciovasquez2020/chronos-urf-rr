# Newstein Diameter-Separation Capacity Surface

Status: OPEN

## Statement

Assume distinct fibers \(u\neq v\), and nonzero local twisted classes
\[
[c_u]\in Z_1(B_r(u);\mathbf F_2)/W_u,
\qquad
[c_v]\in Z_1(B_r(v);\mathbf F_2)/W_v.
\]

The theorem-level target is:
\[
\partial S=c_u-c_v
\Longrightarrow
\operatorname{diam}(\operatorname{supp} S)>L.
\]

Equivalently, no \(L\)-bounded filling identifies nonzero twisted classes from distinct fibers.

## Role

This is the exact theorem-level proof surface for the cross-fiber diameter-separation obstruction.
