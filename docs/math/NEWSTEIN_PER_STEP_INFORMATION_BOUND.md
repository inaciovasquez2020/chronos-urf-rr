# Newstein Per-Step Information Bound

## Status
OPEN

## Statement
Let \((\mathcal H_t)_{t \ge 0}\) be the Newstein filtration/history process attached to the quotient-gap reduction.
There exists a uniform constant \(C < \infty\) such that every admissible Newstein refinement step satisfies
\[
I(X;Y_t \mid \mathcal H_{t-1}) \le C.
\]

## Role
This is the Chronos compatibility bound required to integrate the Newstein reduction into the bounded-information refinement framework.
