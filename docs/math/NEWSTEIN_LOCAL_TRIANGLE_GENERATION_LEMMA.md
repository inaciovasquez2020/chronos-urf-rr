# Newstein Local Triangle-Generation Lemma

## Status
Conditional target.

## Lemma
Let \(x\in V(B_n)\). Assume
\[
L>2r+1,
\qquad
\operatorname{girth}(X_n)>2r+1.
\]

Then every cycle
\[
C\subseteq B_r^{B_n}(x)
\]
lies in the \(\mathbb F_2\)-span of triangle boundaries contained in \(B_r^{B_n}(x)\).

Equivalently,
\[
Z_1\!\bigl(B_r^{B_n}(x)\bigr)
=
\left\langle \partial\tau : \tau \subseteq B_r^{B_n}(x)\ \text{a triangle}\right\rangle_{\mathbb F_2}.
\]

## Consequence
The local triangle-generation lemma supplies item (1) in the Newstein local cycle-vanishing lemma.

## Remaining proof obligations
1. prove a radius-\(r\) ball cannot wrap around a torus gadget when \(L>2r+1\);
2. prove a radius-\(r\) ball cannot contain a backbone cycle when \(\operatorname{girth}(X_n)>2r+1\);
3. prove the resulting local \(2\)-complex is simply connected;
4. deduce that every local \(\mathbb F_2\)-cycle is generated by triangle boundaries.

## Overclaim guard
No proof of the lemma is claimed here.
