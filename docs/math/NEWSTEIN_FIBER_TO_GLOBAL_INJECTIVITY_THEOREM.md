## Statement

\[
\iota_v^{\mathrm{triv}}:
Z_1(\widetilde T_v^{\mathrm{triv}})/W_v^{\mathrm{triv}}
\to
Q(G_n),
\qquad
\iota_v^{\mathrm{tw}}:
Z_1(\widetilde T_v^{\mathrm{tw}})/W_v^{\mathrm{tw}}
\to
Q(H_n)
\]
are injective, and for \(u\neq v\),
\[
\operatorname{im}(\iota_u)\cap \operatorname{im}(\iota_v)=0.
\]
