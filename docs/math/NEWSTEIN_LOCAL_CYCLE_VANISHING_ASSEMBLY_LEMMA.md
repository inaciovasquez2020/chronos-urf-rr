# Newstein Local Cycle-Vanishing Assembly Lemma

## Status
Conditional target.

## Lemma
Assume:

1. the Newstein local triangle-generation lemma;
2. the Newstein triangle-vanishing lemma.

Then for every vertex \(x\in V(B_n)\) and every cycle \(C\subseteq B_r^{B_n}(x)\),
\[
\phi_H(C)=0.
\]

## Consequence
The Newstein local cycle-vanishing assembly lemma discharges the Newstein local cycle-vanishing lemma from its two isolated sublemmas.

## Remaining proof obligations
1. express any local cycle as an \(\mathbb F_2\)-sum of triangle boundaries;
2. apply vanishing of \(\phi_H\) on each triangle boundary;
3. use \(\mathbb F_2\)-linearity to conclude \(\phi_H(C)=0\).

## Overclaim guard
No proof of the lemma is claimed here.
