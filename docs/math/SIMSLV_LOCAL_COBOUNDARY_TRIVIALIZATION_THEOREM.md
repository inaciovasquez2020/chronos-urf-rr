## Statement

Assume
\[
Z_1(B_r(x);\mathbb F_2)
=
\left\langle \partial\tau : \Phi_2(\tau)\right\rangle_{\mathbb F_2}
\]
and
\[
\phi_H(\partial\tau)=0
\quad\text{for every admissible local triangle }\tau.
\]

Then
\[
\phi_H\!\mid_{Z_1(B_r(x))}=0,
\]
so the twisted and trivial rooted-ball witness structures are locally cohomologous, yielding rooted-ball trivialization.
