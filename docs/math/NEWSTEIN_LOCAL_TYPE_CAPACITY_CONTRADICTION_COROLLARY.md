# Newstein Local-Type Capacity Contradiction Corollary

Status: OPEN

## Statement

Assume the Sigma Growth vs Local-Type Capacity package:
\[
\dim_{\mathbf F_2}(Z_1/W^{\mathrm{glob}})\to\infty
\]
along the chosen graph family, while every bounded local-type factorization model would force
\[
\dim_{\mathbf F_2}(Z_1/W^{\mathrm{glob}})\le C(r,k,\Delta).
\]

Then the quotient invariant \(Q\) does not factor through any bounded local type space determined only by \((r,k,\Delta)\).

In particular, \(Q\) does not factor through rooted radius-\(r\) type and does not factor through \(FO^k_r\)-type.

## Role

This is the exact contradiction corollary converting the capacity clash into the final non-factorization statement.
