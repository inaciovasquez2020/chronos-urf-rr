## Statement

Let \(\widetilde T^{\mathrm{triv}}\) denote the trivial double cover fiber and \(\widetilde T^{\mathrm{tw}}\) the twisted double cover fiber. Let \(W^{\mathrm{triv}}\) and \(W^{\mathrm{tw}}\) be the spans of lifted triangle boundaries. Then
\[
\dim_{\mathbb F_2}\!\bigl(Z_1(\widetilde T^{\mathrm{triv}})/W^{\mathrm{triv}}\bigr)=4,
\qquad
\dim_{\mathbb F_2}\!\bigl(Z_1(\widetilde T^{\mathrm{tw}})/W^{\mathrm{tw}}\bigr)=2.
\]
