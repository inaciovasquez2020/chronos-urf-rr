# SiMSLV Triangle Vanishing

Status: OPEN

## Statement

Let \(\phi_H\) be the cocycle assigned to the witness-layer.

For every admissible local triangle
\[
\tau=[u,v,w]\subseteq B_r(x)
\]
with
\[
\Phi_2(u,v,w),
\]
one has
\[
\phi_H(\partial\tau)=0.
\]

## Role

Together with local triangle generation,
\[
Z_1(B_r(x);\mathbb F_2)
=
\left\langle \partial\tau : \Phi_2(\tau)\right\rangle_{\mathbb F_2},
\]
this implies
\[
\phi_H\!\mid_{Z_1(B_r(x))}=0.
\]
