# SiMSLV Ball Simple Connectedness

Status: OPEN

## Assumptions

\[
L>2r+1,
\qquad
\operatorname{girth}(X_n)>2r+1.
\]

## Intended consequence

Under the assumptions above:

1. No rooted radius-\(r\) ball wraps around a torus fiber.
2. No rooted radius-\(r\) ball contains a backbone cycle.
3. The local witness-layer \(2\)-complex inside \(B_r(x)\) is simply connected.

## Role

This is the geometric input that removes global obstructions from the rooted-ball branch and reduces local \(1\)-cycles to sums of triangle boundaries.

## Finish condition

Replace this OPEN file by a proof that every rooted radius-\(r\) ball is simply connected in the witness-layer \(2\)-complex under the stated no-wrap and large-girth assumptions.
