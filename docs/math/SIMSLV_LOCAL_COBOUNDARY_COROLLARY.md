\[

\left\langle \partial\tau : \Phi_2(\tau)\right\rangle_{\mathbb F_2},

\[
\phi_H(\partial\tau)=0

\[

\]

Consequently the rooted-ball twisted/trivial witness structures are locally cohomologous, yielding the intended rooted-ball trivialization step.
