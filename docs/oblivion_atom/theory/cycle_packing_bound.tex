\title{Cycle Packing Bound under FO$^k$ Homogeneity}
\date{}
\maketitle

\section*{Definitions}

Let $G=(V,E)$ be a graph with maximum degree $\Delta$.

Let

\[
C_R(G)
=
\dim_{\mathbb F_2}
\left(
\sum_{v\in V}
\iota_{v*} Z_1(B_R(v))
\right)
\]

Let

\[
T_r(G)
=
\left|
\{
\operatorname{tp}^k_r(v): v\in V
\}
\right|.
\]

\section*{Lemma}

\begin{lemma}
If $T_r(G)=1$, then
\[
C_R(G)\le c(k,\Delta,R)
\]
for a constant depending only on $(k,\Delta,R)$.
\end{lemma}

\begin{proof}

If $T_r(G)=1$, all vertices share the same FO$^k$ $r$-type.

Hence every rooted $R$-ball $B_R(v)$ is isomorphic.

Therefore all local cycle spaces

\[
Z_1(B_R(v))
\]

are identical.

Thus the vectors contributed to

\[
\sum_v \iota_{v*} Z_1(B_R(v))
\]

belong to a fixed finite-dimensional family determined by the
single rooted graph type.

Since $B_R(v)$ contains at most

\[
O(\Delta^R)
\]

edges, its cycle space dimension is bounded.

Hence the total span dimension

\[
C_R(G)
\]

is bounded by a constant

\[
c(k,\Delta,R).
\]

\end{proof}
