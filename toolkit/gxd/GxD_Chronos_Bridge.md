GxD → Chronos Bridge

Let κ be the GxD entropy rate.

Under finite transcript capacity C per refinement step:

Maximum information extracted per step ≤ C.

If total detectable entropy grows at rate κ per unit radius,
then depth lower bound:

Depth ≥ (κ / C) * R.

Thus:

Expansion entropy + bounded capacity ⇒ linear depth growth.

This provides quantitative backbone for Chronos depth lower bounds.
