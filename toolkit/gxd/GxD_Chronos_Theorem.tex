\begin{theorem}[GxD–Chronos Depth Bound]
Let $H$ be finitely generated with growth exponent $\kappa$.
Let $O$ be an observer with $GxD_O(s)=\kappa$.
Assume per-step transcript capacity $\le C$ bits.

Then any refinement algorithm detecting orbit diversity up to radius $R$
must satisfy

\[
\mathrm{Depth} \ge \frac{\kappa}{C} R.
\]

\end{theorem}
