\section*{RG Closure Criterion (2D)}

For block-spin renormalization in two dimensions:

\begin{itemize}
\item Free Gaussian fixed point ($\eta = 0$) exhibits logarithmic obstruction.
\item Interacting fixed point with anomalous dimension $\eta > 0$ yields contraction exponent $L^{-2+\eta}$.
\item Strict $L^{-2}$ closure requires $\eta > 0$.
\end{itemize}

This distinguishes marginal Gaussian behavior from interacting RG contraction.
