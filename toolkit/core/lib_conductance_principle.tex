\section*{Local Information Barrier (LIB) Principle}

Let $(\mathcal S,\pi,P)$ be a reversible Markov chain.
For measurable $A \subset \mathcal S$, define conductance:

\[
\Phi(A) = \frac{Q(A,A^c)}{\pi(A)}.
\]

Barrier functional:

\[
\mathcal B(A) = -\log \Phi(A).
\]

Universal lower bound:

\[
\mathrm{MixingTime} \ge \frac12 e^{\mathcal B(A)}.
\]

This applies to RG, OGP, expander gadgets, and impurity truncation models.
